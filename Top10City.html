महाराष्ट्रातील शीर्ष 10 शहरं

Maharashtra's Top 10 City

Mumbai

Nickname: The City of Dreams, Financial Capital of India
Highlights: Gateway of India, Marine Drive, Bollywood industry, Financial hubs like BSE and NSE.

Pune

Nickname: Oxford of the East, Cultural Capital of Maharashtra
Highlights: Historical landmarks like Shaniwar Wada, IT and education hub, pleasant climate.

Nagpur

Nickname: Orange City, Winter Capital of Maharashtra
Highlights: Deekshabhoomi, Zero Mile Stone, Vidarbha region's administrative hub.

Nashik

Nickname: Wine Capital of India
Highlights: Trimbakeshwar Temple, Kumbh Mela, Sula Vineyards.

Aurangabad

Nickname: City of Gates
Highlights: Ajanta and Ellora Caves, Bibi Ka Maqbara, Industrial hub.

Thane

Nickname: Lake City
Highlights: Numerous lakes, proximity to Mumbai, growing residential and commercial zones.

Solapur

Nickname: Textile City
Highlights: Famous for Solapuri chadars (bedsheets), Siddheshwar Temple.

Kolhapur

Nickname: City of Warriors, Sugar Bowl of India
Highlights: Mahalaxmi Temple, Kolhapuri footwear and cuisine.

Amravati

Nickname: Cultural Hub of Vidarbha
Highlights: Ambadevi Temple, thriving agriculture.

Jalgaon

Nickname: Banana City
Highlights: Leading banana-producing region, Ajanta Caves nearby.
