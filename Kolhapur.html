KOLHAPUR

About Kolhapur

Kolhapur is a culturally rich city known for its deep-rooted traditions and royal Maratha heritage. It is home to the famous Mahalaxmi Temple, which draws thousands of devotees every year. The city was once a princely state and still retains its regal charm through its palaces, museums, and historical architecture.

Kolhapur is equally known for its unique offerings like Kolhapuri chappals, spicy cuisine (especially the famed mutton thali), and contributions to wrestling and classical music. Its markets are a treasure trove of handmade crafts, and its welcoming atmosphere makes it a beloved destination for tourists and pilgrims alike.

Key Information

  • Population: 561,489
  • Languages: Marathi, Hindi
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous For: Footwear, temples, cuisine, wrestling
